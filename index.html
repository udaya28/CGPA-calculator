Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) is

  • SGPA - Semester End Grade Point Average
  • CGPA - Cumulative Grade Point
  • Average SGPA = (Sum of Subject Grade points * Credits)/(Total number of credits per semester)
  • CGPA = (Sum of SGPAs of All Semesters)/ (Number of Semesters)

SGPA stands for Semester Grade Point Average. It is a grading scheme used by many universities. Mathematically, it is the weighted mean of the grades achieved in various subjects with the credits allotted to the subjects acting as weights. To Calculate SGPA, you must know the credits allotted to each subject. The credits are usually mentioned on the syllabus. Then, multiply the credit allotted to each subject with the grade achieved in that subject and add the products. Divide this sum by sum of all credits to get your SGPA.
